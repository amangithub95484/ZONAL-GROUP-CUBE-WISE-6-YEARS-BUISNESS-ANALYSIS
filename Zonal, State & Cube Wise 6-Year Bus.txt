Zonal, State & Cube Wise 6-Year Business Analysis

Professional Business Insight Report

1. Executive Summary

The six-year business analysis evaluates revenue distribution across zones, states, and retail channels (cube types). The analysis identifies key revenue drivers, geographic concentration of sales, and channel performance to support strategic expansion and operational planning.

The findings indicate that North Zone is the dominant revenue contributor, driven largely by high-performing states such as Delhi, Punjab, and Haryana. In terms of retail channels, Exclusive Brand Outlets (EBO) generate the majority of sales, demonstrating strong brand-led retail performance.

5. Business Risk Assessment

2. Channel Dependency

3. Market Penetration Gap

South, East, and North East zones show relatively low business contribution, indicating untapped markets.

7. Key Strategic Recommendations

1️⃣ Expand retail footprint outside North Zone to reduce geographic risk.

2️⃣ Increase Shop-in-Shop partnerships with large retail stores to improve brand visibility.

3️⃣ Improve MBO channel strategy through stronger dealer networks and incentives.

4️⃣ Focus on high-growth states like Maharashtra, Gujarat, and Karnataka.

5️⃣ Leverage data analytics and customer insights to optimize store location strategy.

8. Conclusion

The six-year business analysis clearly highlights that the company's growth has been primarily driven by North Zone markets and Exclusive Brand Outlet channels. While this strategy has delivered strong performance, the next phase of growth will depend on regional diversification and multi-channel retail expansion.

Strategic investments in emerging markets and alternate retail channels could significantly enhance long-term revenue sustainability and market share.
